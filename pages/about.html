About us

People behind the repairs

404 PixelFx started as a two-person repair bench and grew into a team obsessed with getting devices right the first time.

Our story

Started on a repair bench, built on referrals

404 PixelFx opened its doors in 2018 with one goal: make computer repair feel less like a gamble. What began as after-hours fixes for friends turned into a full-service shop handling everything from cracked screens to full network installs.

Today, we serve homes, students, and small businesses across the region — but the approach hasn't changed. Diagnose honestly, explain clearly, fix it properly.

Our Mission

To deliver honest, fast, and skilled repair services that keep people connected to the devices they depend on — without the confusion or the upsell that usually comes with tech support.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted computer repair and support name in the region — known as much for how we treat customers as for the quality of the fix itself.

Goal 01

Keep average repair turnaround under 48 hours without cutting corners.

Goal 02

Expand home-service coverage to every barangay in the province by 2027.

Goal 03

Train and certify every technician on-staff in advanced hardware diagnostics.
